Privacy Policy

Osiris Cloud is mostly compliant under NYU digital privacy rules. However, additional/separate policies apply, which are described below.

This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure your information is kept secure.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for our services, we may collect personal information from NYU such as your name, email address, and affiliation.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data about how you use our services, including IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: We use your personal information to provide, maintain, and improve our cloud services.
  • Communication: We may use your contact information to send you updates, promotional materials, or service-related notices. You can opt out of receiving promotional communications at any time.
  • Security: We use your data to detect, prevent, and respond to security incidents, as well as to protect the integrity of our services.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by NYU and/or public authorities.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You can also object to or restrict certain processing activities. To exercise these rights, please contact us at infra [at] osiriscloud.io

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through our services or via email. Your continued use of our services after such changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
