Osiris Cloud is a self-hosted cloud infrastructure platform created at the NYU OSIRIS Lab for automating and simplifying infrastructure. The original goal of the project was to run CSAW CTF on premise. Osiris Cloud aims to power homes, schools and enterprises to solve their infra problems by providing them with a platform that allows them to reinvent continuously.
