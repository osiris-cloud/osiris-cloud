Frequently Asked Questions

What is Osiris Cloud?

It is an easy-to-use cloud infrastructure platform, with integrated cybersecurity tools.

When will Osiris Cloud be available?

It is currently in alpha. Please submit the interest form to get notified, as when it becomes available.

How does support work?

We believe that a good platform should have the user's back when they need it. Hence, we have human support to answer any questions you have.

What are the free services available?

The free services include a limited amount of storage, compute, and network resources. You can use these resources to deploy your applications. A page will be available soon with more details.
