Frequently Asked Questions

What is Osiris Cloud?

It is like any other cloud, but made by students for students. it offers a lot of services and can be used free of charge. It is in active development.

Who is this for?

Osiris Cloud is for NYU students and faculty who need a cloud service for their projects, homework, or other hobbies.

How can I use this?

Currently, it is available on a request basis. Please email us at infra [at] osiriscloud.io to get access.

How can I get support?

On our Discord!
